About the Candidate

Name: Sherrie Cohen

Office: Philadelphia Municipal Court Judge

Political Party: Democrat

Sherrie Cohen is a tenant-rights attorney and lifelong advocate for racial, social, and economic justice. She has spent decades fighting for working families, fair housing, LGBTQ+ rights, and accessible city services. As a democratic socialist and community organizer, Sherrie is committed to ensuring fairness, integrity, and justice for every resident who steps into the courtroom.
